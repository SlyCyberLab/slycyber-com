## Hey, I'm Emsly

IT Administrator by day. Homelab builder by night. Based in the US

I've been in IT for 4+ years, started in frontline support, worked my way into System Administrator. My day job at a material handling and software company has me wearing a lot of hats: sysadmin, CMMC compliance, and the person who gets called when something breaks at 8am.

## The Homelab

Outside of work I run a homelab I call **The Forge** — two Proxmox servers, a full Docker stack, Tailscale mesh networking, and an isolated security lab called **Citadel** that I use to practice detection engineering and map controls to NIST CSF 2.0.

It started as a way to learn. It became an obsession.

## This Site

I write about all of it here, not step-by-step tutorials, but the real story of building things, breaking them, and figuring out what actually matters. Topics include:

- Homelab infrastructure and self-hosted services
- SIEM, detection engineering, and security tooling
- CMMC and NIST compliance work
- Automation and scripting
